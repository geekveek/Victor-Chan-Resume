Description

A Cat and Mouse Game

Blue Cheese is a player v.s AI strategy game revolving around the idea of chasing and seeking through different levels with different obstacles and structures. Each player will have different roles and attributes attached to them base on their character. You take the role of Carter, the cat, to gain points by killing Mini, the mouse.

Story

Character: Carter the Cat

Carter's objective is to kill Mini before he finds the cheese. He can collect resources around the map for his traps, which he can place around the map to trap Mini. When Mini is in his line of sight, Carter can shoot and kill Mini to gain points.

Character: Mini the Mouse

Mini's objective is to find the cheese. At the same time, he has to avoid falling into Carter's traps and being shot by him. Mini can collect resources around the map for his stun gun, which he can then use to stun Carter for a short while and thus given the chance to escape from Carter.

The Flow

Carter and Mini will be placed at different parts of the map where they will both try to achieve their individual objectives. The game ends when there is no time remaining.

Background Story

The world of cats is a ruthless one. It is where success was measured mainly by mouse kill count. Mouse kills give recognition amongst peers, and leads to receiving job requests from human employers who see your kill count as a resume for competence.

Carter decends from THE most reputed cat family in the kitty world, the "Nekolite". Carter's grandfather, who was once known by one and all as the "Master Catcher", had his whole career ruined by he who went by the name of "The Flash", the mouse who had evaded capture for over a 100 years while wreaking havoc in human households everywhere. The Flash was the Master Catcher's one and only blemish in his otherwise perfect capture rate, and the latter passed away due to old age without ever beating the Flash in a 1 to 1 match.

Other cats used this record to make jokes against the Nekolite family for being eternal failures after so many years of boasting a perfect capture rate. Carter, being a hot headed cat by nature, took these insults to heart and grew up habouring hatred against not only the Milako tribe, where the Flash originated from, but also against the kitty world for undermining the abilities of his dear family. He made a vow to master every technique known to the Nekolite family and use them to, one day, eliminate the Milako tribe once and for all.

Mini is the young trainee mouse that was born into the famed Milako tribe and slated to take over the throne after his father passes on. He was taught by his master not to hate cats for taking the lives of their kind, but instead to search for a peaceful resolution for both parties. As a result, Mini does not believe in the act of killing cats, because he is well aware that it only perpetuates the vicious cycle of war and suffering currently going on.

His master tells him on day, "I have no more to teach you, young rodent. You have been one of my brightest students, and I know that one day, one day you will be able to bring peace to the land. Venture into the outside world, young rodent. FInd the parts of the legendary Blue Cheese and piece them together. The Blue Cheese will tell you exactly what to do. Seek to understand, not to hate. I will always be with you in spirit." As such, young Mini sets off on a quest in search of this peaceful resolution that his master so desires.

Carter and Mini crossed paths on a stormy night in the battlefield. "You!", shouted Carter. "Are you one of the Milako mouse tribe?" Not knowing anything, Mini innocently replied, "Yes, what of it?" "At last. I have searched the land far and wide for a chance to kill you and your people. Now that I've found you, there is no escape. You will pay for what you've done to my grandfather!" Carter's eyes were blazing with anticipation and fury. "This must be the hatred that my master spoke of." thought Mini. "I don't even know what he's talking about! What should I do?"

What will become of Mini? Will he be able to convince Carter to put his hatred down and seek the inner peace that he himself posseses? Or will he succumb to Carter's overwhelming desire for vengence? And what will the Blue Cheese reveal?

Features

Mini (played by the AI) is an intelligent mouse that relies on fuzzy logic and smart exploration algorithm to find the cheese. He is skilled at escaping and avoiding Carter (played by the player). Mini has a stun gun that he will decide to use base on a fuzzy logic algorithm.

However, Carter is not handicap as he has his own mouse traps and weapons to kill the mouse. Overall, it is a fair game and the winner is decided based on the points accumulated.
